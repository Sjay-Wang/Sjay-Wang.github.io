Short Bio

I am a PhD Candidate in the department of Computing of The Hong Kong Polytechnic University (PolyU), where I am fortunate to be advised by Assistant Professor Fan Wenqi and IEEE fellow Qing Li.

I got BSc degree in Information and Computing Science from Xi’an Jiaotong-Liverpool University in China and the University of Liverpool in the UK in July, 2022.

I am now working on Robustness and Graph Neural Networks as well as their applications in recommender systems.
